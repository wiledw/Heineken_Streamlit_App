13.5 Subsequent events

At the end of October 2020, HEINEKEN announced the acquisition of cider brand Strongbow from Asahi
Group Holdings Limited (Asahi) in Australia, along with two other cider brands, Little Green and Bonamy’s.
As part of the transaction, HEINEKEN will also gain the perpetual licenses on beer brands Stella Artois and
Beck’s in Australia.The transaction was completed on 6 January 2021 for €137 million.
